About

A practitioner who ships.

I have spent over fifteen years teaching English to young learners and teenagers — first in Egypt, now in a national middle school in Riyadh. Time in the room taught me what actually moves a learner forward, and where ordinary materials fall short.

So I started building. Under my Learn to Impact brand I design self-contained, offline-capable learning apps and classroom-ready document kits — exam prep, reading and grammar units, model-answer libraries — each grounded in a named pedagogical framework rather than guesswork.

Pedagogy tells me what to build. Engineering lets me build it at scale — thousands of questions, not a worksheet at a time.

I am completing an MA in TEFL at Arab Open University (Cairo) and now working toward a full transition into the EdTech industry, where classroom credibility and the ability to actually build the product are still a rare pairing.
